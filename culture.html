HANOVER

Hanover is located on the northwest coast of Jamaica.
It is the second smallest parish and it's capital is Lucea. In the past, Lucea was an old maritime town and famous sea-port where sailors spent brief periods before setting sail to other ports

WESTMORELAND

Westmoreland is located on the southwest coast of Jamaica. Westmoreland is probably so called because it is the most westerly Parish in Jamaica
