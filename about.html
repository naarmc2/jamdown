ABOUT

Jamdown is a website dedicated to Jamaicans abroad that touches on the island's history and culture, has a a likkle interview section with responses from Jamaicans in my community, mentions Jamaican businesses inside and outside of Jamaica (specifically in the area of New York). In addition it, has a blog for open discussion about topics. The website features some photos from the internet. The purpose of this website is to connect those both abroad and home with one another while providing those who are away from the island with the feeling that they still reside there. Similar to how I felt disconnected from Jamaica when I migrated, other immigrant Jamaicans might feel the same way and perhaps this would help them.
